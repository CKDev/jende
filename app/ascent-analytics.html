Ascent Analytics

Jende’s analytical model has been honed and validated over many years at University of Texas medical institutions. It creates whole-institution, real-time reporting across a spectrum of data sources and can be easily customized.

Unify Institutional Data

With a validated model and plug & play libraries. Leverage the Analytical Model designed for Higher Education at scale. Jende’s Ascent Analytics create dashboards and reports that serve as a bridge between critical, disparate data sets. Reports are self-service and real-time, rendered through Power BI.

 

Plug and Play Libraries

Ascent Analytics connects natively to data sources like Oracle, Oracle Hyperion, Oracle PeopleSoft, Epic & Clarity, UTIMCO, Press Ganey and much more.

Proven Track Record

Validated over five years at the University of Texas Medical Branch and UT System, Ascent has contributed to an aggregate $110M savings at some of the most prestigious institutions in the country,

Enhances, Doesn’t Replace

The Ascent Analytical model uses flexible and secure Azure-based cloud technologies. It extends your institution’s current capabilities without leading to laborious platform replacement decisions.
