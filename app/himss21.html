The Ascent Advantage

Turn a mountain of institutional data into meaningful actionable insights. Jende’s comprehensive, integrated analytical model has been honed and validated over many years at University of Texas medical institutions. Ascent Analytics creates whole-institution, real-time reporting across a spectrum of data sources and can be easily customized.

Use Ascent to optimize and facilitate decisions in all aspects of compensation planning, budgeting, and strategic planning.


Next-Level Institutional Data Analytics

CAMPSITE (Comprehensive Asset Management for Performance), is an integrated data management system that supports personnel management from job creation, through onboarding, to productivity evaluations.

Its robust reporting, offers approximately 150 distinct reports for auditing and proactive decision making. Use CAMPSITE for capacity planning, pay equity initiatives, and performance awards.

It uses the Ascent Analytics model and data pipeline to consolidate information across the institution to automate workflows for processes unique to medical centers, including faculty recruitment, compensation planning, and staff evaluations.
